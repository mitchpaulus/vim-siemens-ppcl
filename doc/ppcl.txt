*Functions*


MAX(result,pt1,...,pt15)            *MAX*


TABLE(input,output,x1,y1,...,x7,y7)   *TABLE*

input = (x)
output = (y)
x1,y1,...,x7,y7 = pairs of points defining the table, values are
interpolated in between. Must be put in ascending order by x.
